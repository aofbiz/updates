Docs / Academy / Quick Start

Getting Started

Welcome to AOF Biz. Master the core workflow of your business management in under 5 minutes.

1

Brand Your Space

Go to Settings > Profile. Upload your high-resolution logo and set your business tagline. This branding will appear on all generated invoices and quotations.

2

Define Products

Navigate to Settings > Products. Organize your catalog by creating categories (e.g., "Wooden Frames") and adding individual items with their default pricing.

3

Create Your First Order

Head to the Orders module. Use the intelligent "New Order" form which auto-fills product details and calculates totals instantly.

4

Sync & Share

Once an order is saved, use the WhatsApp icon to send a professional PDF invoice directly to your client. Everything syncs across your devices automatically.

Docs / Modules / Dashboard

Dashboard & Analytics

Your business command center, providing real-time visibility into your operational health.

Key Metrics

  • Net Sales: Total revenue generated from orders marked as 'Paid'.
  • Net Profit: The core indicator of your success. Calculated as Total Revenue - Total Expenses.
  • Success Rate: Percentage of orders successfully delivered without returns.

Dashboard charts refresh every time you enter the app, ensuring you're always looking at the latest data.

Docs / Modules / Inventory

Inventory Management

Real-time stock tracking that ensures you never miss a sale due to missing materials.

Active Tracking

Inventory in AOF Biz is "Active". When you process an order, the system automatically deducts the necessary stock from your inventory based on the items sold.

Smart Alerts

Set Reorder Points for every item. When stock levels fall below this threshold, the system triggers a low-stock alert on your dashboard.

Pro Tip: Use the 'Bulk Update' feature to quickly adjust stock levels after receiving a new shipment.

Expense Tracking

Monitor your operational costs to get a precise view of your business's true profitability.

Docs / Settings / Logistics

Tracking Numbers

Optimize your shipping workflow by pre-loading courier tracking stickers.

Sequential Assignment

Instead of manual entry for every order, you can upload a massive list of tracking IDs (e.g., from a sticker roll). The system will automatically assign the next available ID whenever an order is moved to 'Dispatched'.

Docs / Settings / General

Business Identity

Configure how your brand appears to your customers.

Profile Configuration

  • Business Name: Displayed prominently on invoices and the app sidebar.
  • Slogan: A short tagline appearing below your business name.
  • Logo: Upload a square PNG/SVG (max 2MB). We recommend 256x256px for optimal clarity.

Operational Defaults

  • Default Delivery Charge: Automatically added to new orders.
  • Quotation Expiry: Validity period for quotes (days).
  • Print Page Size: A4, A5, or Letter.

Docs / Support / FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries.

Can I use AOF Biz offline?

Yes. AOF Biz is designed with a "Local-First" approach. You can manage everything offline, and the app will sync your changes to the cloud once you regain internet access (Pro users only).

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for both local storage and cloud sync. Your data is purely yours and never shared.

Docs / Support / Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting

Resolving common technical issues.

Sync is stuck

Check your internet connection and ensure you are logged into the correct account. If the problem persists, try a manual 'Force Sync' from Settings.

Invoices not downloading

Ensure your browser has permission to trigger downloads. On mobile apps, ensure the app has storage permissions enabled.
