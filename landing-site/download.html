Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to download?

Yes. AOF Biz runs locally on your machine. We do not upload your sensitive business data to any cloud unless you explicitly enable backups.

Can I use it offline?

Absolutely. The core features work entirely offline. An internet connection is only needed for initial setup, updates, and cloud backups.

How do I update?

The application automatically checks for updates on startup. When a new version is available, you'll be prompted to install it with a single click.
